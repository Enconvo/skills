## STYLE-01 — Strategy Consulting

**Vibe:** McKinsey, BCG, Bain. Data-first, no-nonsense. Every cell earns its place.

- **Fonts:** Georgia (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells). No decorative fonts.
- **Colors:** Navy `#0B1D3A` headings, `#1A1A1A` body, `#999999` muted, `#003DA5` accent (royal blue), white sheet bg.
- **Column Setup:** Auto-width with 2-char padding. Right-align numerics. Freeze row 1 (headers).
- **Cover Sheet:** Centered title in row 10, thin blue accent border below, metadata (date, author, version) in rows below.
- **Data Tables:** Blue header row `#003DA5` + white text. Alternating `#F5F5F5` rows. Hairline borders only. Auto-filter enabled.
- **KPI Cards:** Merged cells with blue top border. Large metric value centered, label above in muted gray.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Green `#16A34A` for positive deltas, red `#DC2626` for negative. Data bars in blue.
- **Design notes:** Restrained. Max 2 accent colors. No images. Precise number formatting. White space via empty columns.

## STYLE-02 — Executive Editorial

**Vibe:** Harvard Business Review, The Economist. Thought-leadership elegance.

- **Fonts:** Rockwell (headings, bold) + Georgia (data cells). Italic Georgia for annotations.
- **Colors:** Crimson `#C8102E` headings, `#333333` body, `#8C8279` muted, cream accents `#FAF7F2`, warm white bg.
- **Column Setup:** Wide columns with generous padding. Left-align text, right-align numbers.
- **Cover Sheet:** Maximum whitespace, crimson accent underline below title, author info at bottom.
- **Data Tables:** Crimson top border on header, no vertical lines, clean horizontal separators. Alt-row `#FAF7F2`.
- **KPI Cards:** Warm cream bg `#FAF7F2` with crimson left border (via cell border). Large metric in bold.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Crimson icon sets for trends. Subtle color scales (cream to crimson).
- **Design notes:** Generous whitespace via row height. Crimson accents sparingly. Section separators via thick borders.

## STYLE-03 — Creative Brief

**Vibe:** Sketch-pad, brainstorm, agency creative. Loose, informal, energetic.

- **Fonts:** Comic Sans MS (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells). Italic Calibri for annotations.
- **Colors:** Dark pencil `#3C3C3C` headings, `#3C3C3C` body, `#A0A0A0` muted, rust orange `#D46A3C` accent, ink blue `#2B4570`, kraft bg `#F5F0E1`.
- **Column Setup:** Irregular widths (deliberate). Narrow labels, wide content columns.
- **Cover Sheet:** Rust orange accent border on left column. Hand-drawn-feel title. Kraft-tone fill on cover area.
- **Data Tables:** Thin borders only `#A0A0A0`. No header fills — just bold text. Sketchy feel.
- **KPI Cards:** Kraft bg `#F5F0E1` fill + thin pencil-grey border. Bold orange metric values.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Orange highlight for key items. Minimal — let data speak.
- **Design notes:** Loose layout. Intentionally imperfect column widths. Annotation-style italic notes in adjacent cells.

## STYLE-04 — Playful / Kawaii

**Vibe:** Kid-friendly, lifestyle brand, fun product docs. Bubbly, colorful, rounded.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells, 11pt). Rounded, friendly feel.
- **Colors:** Deep plum `#4A2040` headings/body, `#A0A0A0` muted. Rotating pastel accents: pink `#FFD6E0`, lavender `#E8D5F5`, mint `#D5F5E3`. Peach `#FFAB91`, sky `#81D4FA`.
- **Column Setup:** Standard widths with extra row height (generous vertical padding).
- **Cover Sheet:** Pastel pink header area + plum text. Rounded-feel via merged cells with pastel fills.
- **Data Tables:** Pastel header fill (rotating colors per table). Extra padding. No harsh borders — light pastel borders.
- **KPI Cards:** Pastel bg (rotating) + slightly darker border. Large playful metric values.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Pastel color scales. Soft green/pink for positive/negative.
- **Design notes:** Generous row heights. Soft colors. Alternate pastel fills across sections. Never harsh or angular.

## STYLE-05 — Corporate Modern

**Vibe:** Apple, Stripe, modern SaaS. Clean, precise, systematic.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells). Systematic, no-fuss.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#1D1D1F` headings/body, `#6E6E73` muted, electric blue `#0071E3` accent, `#D1D1D6` borders, white bg, alt bg `#F8F9FA`.
- **Column Setup:** Precise, calculated widths. Uniform column groups. Freeze panes on header row.
- **Cover Sheet:** Blue accent bar (merged cell fill), clean hierarchy, systematic metadata layout.
- **Data Tables:** Blue header `#0071E3` + white text. `#F8F9FA` alt rows. Subtle `#D1D1D6` borders. Auto-filter.
- **KPI Cards:** White bg + `#D1D1D6` border + blue top accent border. Large blue metric values.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Blue data bars. Green/red icon sets for trends. Color scales in blue gradient.
- **Design notes:** Card-style sections via bordered ranges. Systematic spacing. KPI cards prominent. Subtle borders throughout.

## STYLE-06 — Bold Narrative

**Vibe:** Cinematic, dramatic, story-driven. Annual reports, brand stories.

- **Fonts:** Arial Black (headings) + Calibri (data cells). Large dramatic titles.
- **Colors:** Deep twilight `#1A1A40` dark elements, warm highlight `#FFF8E1`, cerulean `#1E88E5`, rose `#E91E63`, amber `#FFB300`. White sheet bg.
- **Column Setup:** Wide columns for narrative text alongside data. Bold section headers span full width.
- **Cover Sheet:** Dark navy banner area (merged cells with `#1A1A40` fill + white text). Dramatic presence.
- **Data Tables:** Dark header `#1A1A40` + white text. Clean horizontal rules only. No vertical borders.
- **KPI Cards:** Dark navy bg `#1A1A40` + amber text. Inverted style for drama.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Amber highlights for key data. Rose for warnings. Bold color scales.
- **Design notes:** Wide cinematic feel via extra-wide columns. Rich atmospheric colors. Large dramatic titles. Minimal data density per sheet.

## STYLE-07 — Warm Organic

**Vibe:** Sustainability, wellness, nature, artisan. Earthy, gentle, grounded.

- **Fonts:** Cambria (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells). Warm serif + clean sans.
- **Colors:** Deep brown `#2D1B0E` headings, `#44382C` body, `#8B7355` muted, terracotta `#C2704E` accent, sage green `#7C9A6E`, warm cream `#FDF8F0`.
- **Column Setup:** Standard widths. Generous row heights for breathing room.
- **Cover Sheet:** Minimal elegant. Terracotta accent line, earthy tones, cream fill areas.
- **Data Tables:** Terracotta top border on header. No harsh grid. Warm alt-row `#FDF8F0`. Thin `#D4C5B0` borders.
- **KPI Cards:** Cream bg `#FEF7ED` + terracotta left border. Warm, inviting metric display.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Sage green for positive, terracotta for negative. Warm color scales.
- **Design notes:** Generous whitespace. Warm tones throughout. Two-color accent system (terracotta + sage). Nothing harsh.

## STYLE-08 — Magazine Editorial

**Vibe:** Vogue, Monocle, Bloomberg Businessweek. Asymmetric, bold, typographic.

- **Fonts:** Georgia (headings, bold, 14-16pt) + Georgia (data cells, 10pt). Captions in Calibri 9pt.
- **Colors:** Near-black `#0A0A0A` headings/body, `#757575` muted, yellow `#FFD600` accent, magenta `#E91E63`, cobalt `#1A237E`.
- **Column Setup:** Asymmetric — wider left columns, narrower right. Magazine-layout feel.
- **Cover Sheet:** Bold massive title (16pt+). High contrast black + yellow accent. One bold statement.
- **Data Tables:** Thick top rule `#0A0A0A`, thin bottom rule. No internal vertical borders. Caption below in muted.
- **KPI Cards:** Black bg `#0A0A0A` + white text (inverted) or yellow bg + black text. Maximum contrast.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Yellow highlights for emphasis. Bold icon sets. High-contrast data bars.
- **Design notes:** Asymmetric column widths. High contrast. One bold accent per sheet. Thick divider borders. Mix large headers with small data.

## STYLE-09 — Technical Documentation

**Vibe:** API docs, engineering specs, data dictionaries. Structured, scannable, precise.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells). Consolas for code/ID columns.
- **Colors:** Dark gray `#2C2C2C` headings/body, `#757575` muted, red `#E53935` accent, yellow `#FFEB3B` highlight, warm white `#F0EDE8` for code cells.
- **Column Setup:** Precise calculated widths. ID columns narrow, description columns wide. Freeze panes.
- **Cover Sheet:** Clean hierarchy. Version number prominent. No decorative imagery. Red accent line.
- **Data Tables:** Banded rows with thin borders. Code-style cells (Consolas font, warm-white bg).
- **KPI Cards:** Three types: Info (blue border), Warning (yellow bg tint), Error (red bg tint).
- **Conditional Formatting:** Red for errors/critical. Yellow for warnings. Green for pass/success. Status-code coloring.
- **Design notes:** Scannable. Deep header hierarchy via indented merged cells. Generous use of code-font columns. Numbered sections.

## STYLE-10 — Dashboard Report

**Vibe:** KPI dashboards, data reports, analytics summaries. Metric-forward, structured.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, bold) + Calibri (data cells). Large metrics in Calibri bold 18-24pt.
- **Colors:** Dark `#1C1C1E` elements on white sheet. Blue `#0071E3`, green `#34C759`, purple `#AF52DE`, orange `#FF9F0A` for metric accents.
- **Column Setup:** Narrow margins. Dense but organized grid. KPI sections use wider merged cells.
- **Cover Sheet:** Minimal — title + date + key metric hero (one big number in merged area).
- **Data Tables:** KPI card rows via merged cells with colored top borders. Banded data tables with narrow rows.
- **KPI Cards:** Prominent. Thick colored top border per card. Metric label + big value + change indicator.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Color-coded metrics (green up `#34C759`, red down `#FF3B30`). Sparkline-style data bars.
- **Design notes:** Metric-forward. Each sheet tells a data story. Mixed merged-cell sizes for cards. Color-coded everything. Dense but never cluttered.

## STYLE-11 — Portfolio / Gallery

**Vibe:** Photography, design portfolio, lookbook. Image-forward, minimal text.

- **Fonts:** Calibri (headings, light/thin weight) + Calibri (data cells, small). Caption style throughout.
- **Colors:** `#1A1A1A` headings, `#757575` body/captions, very light gray `#EAEAEA` borders, white bg, red accent `#E60023` sparingly.
- **Column Setup:** Wide columns for image references/paths. Narrow metadata columns. Minimal borders.
- **Cover Sheet:** Full-width title area. Clean, minimal. Red accent underline only.
- **Data Tables:** Invisible borders — minimal grid lines. Clean, gallery-like presentation.
- **KPI Cards:** Minimal text boxes. Thin left border + small italic caption. Never busy.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Minimal. Only subtle highlights for selected/featured items.
- **Design notes:** Minimal text, maximum whitespace. Small, elegant captions. Generous row heights. All about clean data presentation.

---

## STYLE-12 — Retro / Vintage

**Vibe:** Indie zine, risograph, letterpress. Textured, limited palette, imperfect charm.

- **Fonts:** Rockwell (headings, bold) + Consolas (data cells — typewriter feel). Monospace for data.
- **Colors:** Dark navy `#1B2838` (NO pure black), riso blue `#0078BF`, riso red `#FF665E`, riso green `#00A95C`, uncoated paper `#F2E8D5` bg tint.
- **Column Setup:** Uniform column widths (typewriter grid feel). Fixed-width aesthetic.
- **Cover Sheet:** Thick riso-blue border on left column area. Bold Rockwell title. Paper-tone fill `#F2E8D5`.
- **Data Tables:** Thick navy borders `#1B2838`. No fills — just strong lines. Typewriter body text in Consolas.
- **KPI Cards:** Paper bg `#F2E8D5` + thick navy border (all sides). Typewriter text inside.
- **Conditional Formatting:** Riso blue/red/green only. Bold flat color fills. Limited palette.
- **Design notes:** Max 2-3 colors per sheet. No pure black — use dark navy `#1B2838`. Bold flat shapes. Typewriter body creates the retro feel.
